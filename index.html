Our Story

A Property Born
from The Land

Named after three ancient baobab trees that have stood sentinel over the shoreline for centuries, Mbuyu Watatu is an intimate sanctuary where the rhythms of the Indian Ocean set the pace of your days. Part of The Bush Collection, we blend barefoot luxury with a deep respect for the coast.

Private Villas
Ocean-facing retreats designed to dissolve the boundary between inside and out.
Dedicated Team
Your personal concierge anticipates every wish before you voice it.
Farm-to-Table Dining
Fresh catches and local harvests transformed into coastal masterpieces.
Pure Seclusion
A private stretch of white sand where the horizon belongs only to you.

What Awaits

Craft Your Perfect Day

From dawn dhow sails to moonlit bonfires, every hour at Mbuyu Watatu is shaped by the sea.

01
Scuba Diving

Descend into the living reefs of the Shimoni Channel — one of East Africa's most pristine dive sites. Encounter whale sharks, manta rays, and kaleidoscopic coral walls in waters that few ever explore.

02
Reef Snorkelling

Discover a world of coral gardens and tropical fish in the crystal-clear waters just steps from your villa.

03
Sundowner Ritual

Sip handcrafted cocktails as the sun dissolves into the horizon from your private fire-lit beach setup.
